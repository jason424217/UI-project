Welcome to basketball court!
In this web app, we will learn basic concept of basketball’s 5 positions, their duty and overall position sketch in a court.
In the learning section, we will teach you 5 position on court: Point Guard (PG), Shooting Guard (SG), Small Forward (SF), Power Forward (PF), Center (C), and how they collaborate as a team.

In the quiz section, we will be tested on the information you just learned in the form of multiple choice and drag and drop questions and receive a score.
